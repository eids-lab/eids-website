Research & Job Opportunities

PhD Position in ML for IoT

We are seeking a highly motivated PhD student to work on machine learning solutions for IoT applications, with a focus on edge computing and distributed systems.

Application Deadline: May 15, 2025

Start Date: September 2025

  • MSc in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
  • Strong background in machine learning and distributed systems
  • Programming experience in Python and/or C++
  • Experience with ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
  • Full tuition coverage
  • Competitive stipend ($30,000/year)
  • Conference travel funding
  • Access to state-of-the-art computing resources

To apply, please send the following to applications@eids-lab.org:

  • CV/Resume
  • Research statement (max 2 pages)
  • Academic transcripts
  • Contact information for 2-3 references

Summer Research Internship

Join our lab for a 12-week summer research internship focused on edge intelligence and distributed systems for IoT applications.

Application Deadline: March 30, 2025

Duration: June 1 - August 20, 2025

  • Currently enrolled undergraduate or graduate student
  • Background in computer science or electrical engineering
  • Experience with at least one programming language
  • Interest in IoT, distributed systems, or machine learning
  • $8,000 stipend for the summer
  • On-campus housing available
  • Hands-on research experience
  • Mentorship from faculty and graduate students

Apply through our online portal at eids-lab.org/internships with:

  • Resume
  • Statement of interest (500 words max)
  • Academic transcript (unofficial is acceptable)
  • One letter of recommendation

Postdoctoral Fellowship

Two-year postdoctoral fellowship position focused on developing novel edge computing algorithms for distributed IoT systems.

Application Deadline: Rolling basis until filled

Start Date: Flexible, preferred before August 2025

  • PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
  • Strong publication record in relevant conferences/journals
  • Experience with embedded systems and distributed computing
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • Salary: $65,000-$75,000 per year based on experience
  • Comprehensive benefits package
  • Research and travel budget
  • Mentorship and career development opportunities

To apply, email the following to postdoc@eids-lab.org:

  • CV with publication list
  • Research statement (2-3 pages)
  • Three representative publications
  • Contact information for three references
