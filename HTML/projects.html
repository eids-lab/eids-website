This project focuses on developing novel machine learning algorithms for genomic data analysis, with applications in disease prediction and personalized medicine.

  • Create interpretable machine learning models for clinical applications
  • Validate results through collaboration with medical institutions

Quantum Computing Algorithms

Research on quantum algorithms for solving computationally intensive problems, with focus on optimization and cryptography applications.

  • Design quantum algorithms with provable speedup over classical alternatives
  • Simulate quantum algorithms on classical hardware
  • Implement and test on quantum computing testbeds

Principal Investigator: Dr. Robert Chen

Research Assistants: Sarah Johnson, Michael Lee

Student Researchers: 2 PhD, 3 Undergrad

1. Chen, R. & Johnson, S. (2024). "Efficient quantum algorithms for graph problems."
