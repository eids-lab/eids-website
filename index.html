Mission Statement

To advance technological frontiers by developing innovative solutions at the intersection of IoT, artificial intelligence, and distributed computing, enabling smarter, more efficient industrial and technological ecosystems.

Our vision is to become a leading research center that bridges the gap between academic research and industrial applications, fostering collaboration and innovation across disciplines.

Research Areas

Industrial IoT Systems

Developing next-generation IoT architectures for industrial applications, focusing on reliability, scalability, and security for critical infrastructure.

AI at the Edge

Investigating methods to deploy complex machine learning models on resource-constrained edge devices, enabling real-time inference with minimal latency.

Distributed Computing

Exploring novel approaches to distributed systems that can dynamically adapt to changing network conditions while maintaining robust operation.

Sustainable Technology

Researching energy-efficient computing paradigms and sustainable technological solutions to reduce the environmental impact of modern computing infrastructure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join your research lab?

+

We welcome motivated students and researchers! Prospective PhD students should apply through the university's graduate admission portal and mention our lab in their research statement. For postdoc and research assistant positions, please email your CV and research interests to research@iotlab.edu. Undergraduate students interested in internships should check our Opportunities page for current openings.

Does the lab offer industry partnerships?

+

Yes, we actively collaborate with industry partners on research projects related to IoT, edge computing, and smart manufacturing. We offer sponsored research opportunities, technical consulting, and technology transfer. For partnership inquiries, please contact our lab director at director@iotlab.edu to discuss potential collaboration models.

What equipment and facilities does your lab have?

+

Our lab is equipped with state-of-the-art IoT testbeds, edge computing hardware, industrial sensing equipment, and high-performance computing resources. We maintain specialized workstations for AI model development and testing, a small-scale manufacturing simulation environment, and collaborative workspaces for team projects. Our facilities are regularly updated to support cutting-edge research in our focus areas.

How can I access your research publications?

+

Most of our publications are available through our Publications page, where you can download pre-print versions. For papers that aren't publicly accessible, please email the corresponding author listed on the publication. We're committed to open science and strive to make our research findings accessible to the broader community whenever possible.
